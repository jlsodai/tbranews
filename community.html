Beyond the Leader: Leadership that Endures

Zahnri van Geuns
Chief Talent Partner
TheBoardroom Africa


Leadership that endures isn’t built on volume or visibility, it’s shaped quietly, through trust, values, and the courage to build culture before chasing outcomes.

In this piece, Zahnri van Geuns reflects on the leaders who’ve left the greatest impact: those who empower others, hold space for growth, and lead with integrity even when it’s hard.

This philosophy is at the heart of how we approach leadership at TheBoardroom Africa, and why we’re thrilled to welcome Zahnri as our new Chief Talent Partner. She brings over a decade of executive search experience and a track record of placing purpose-driven leaders across the continent. In her new role, she will lead our Leadership Solutions & Advisory practice, deepening our commitment to strategic, long-term partnerships that shape leadership from the inside out.

Featured Opportunity
Closing on 13 June, 2025

African Collaborative

Industry: Venture Philanthropy


The African Collaborative tackles systemic funding disparities that limit access to resources for African-led social impact organisations. Although global philanthropic funding is substantial, these organisations still receive only a small share. Through multi-year unrestricted grants and tailored portfolio support, the initiative ensures African leaders gain the tools and visibility needed to drive lasting systems change. With a core focus on proximity, equity, and sustainability, it bridges funding gaps and shifts power toward locally-driven solutions.

To help advance this mission, the African Collaborative is seeking a passionate, committed individual to join its Board of Directors, someone with expertise in financial management, fundraising, or strong ties to the African philanthropy ecosystem.
