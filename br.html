MDaaS Global

Non-Executive Director


Industry: Healthcare

MDaaS, founded in 2016, is a healthcare company that builds and operates modern, technology-enabled diagnostic centres for underserved communities in Nigeria. Initially selling medical equipment, they pivoted to diagnostics in 2017 after recognising the severe shortage of such services in Africa. Today, they provide radiology, cardiac, and laboratory services to over 120,000 patients through 66,000 tests conducted across their 17 centres, which include full-service, embedded, and sample collection centres in eight states. MDaaS's flagship centre in Ibadan spearheaded their expansion, improving healthcare access for millions in Nigeria.

Mdaas Global is searching for one (1) Non-Executive Director to join their board.

The application deadline for this role is 20 June at midnight, 2024.

Fastizers Food and Confectionery Limited

Non-Executive Director


Industry: FMCG

Fastizers Food and Confectionery Limited (FFCL), established in 2010, is a dynamic Nigerian food manufacturing company accredited by the National Agency for Food and Drugs Administration and Control (NAFDAC). Renowned for their flagship product, Fun Cookies, which has become a staple in Nigerian households, FFCL also offers popular brands like Nibit Mini Snacks and Bitty Cookies. The company has garnered widespread acceptance among major distributors and wholesalers, boasting a distribution network that spans 31 states and includes over 100 distributors nationwide.

Fastizers Food and Confectionery Limited is searching for one (1) Non-Executive Director to join their board.

The application deadline for this role is 20 June at midnight, 2024.

First Housing Company (Tanzania) Limited

Independent Non-Executive Director


Industry: Housing Finance

Since October 2017, First Housing Company (Tanzania) Limited has established itself as a leading financial institution specialising in residential mortgages, licensed by the Bank of Tanzania.

Committed to offering enduring housing solutions to Tanzanian residents, they pioneer the housing finance sector, offering a diverse array of mortgage products finely tuned for home construction, purchase, improvement, and beyond. Their vision is to become the nation's preferred mortgage provider, renowned for both rapid processing of mortgage applications and unmatched service standards.

First Housing Company (Tanzania) Limited is searching for one (1) Independent Non-Executive Director to join their board.

The application deadline for this role is 30 May at midnight, 2024.
