Message from the CEO

Happy New Year,

This year marks ten years since TheBoardroom Africa began. Saying that out loud still feels significant. What started as a bold idea has grown into a trusted institution operating at the intersection of leadership, governance, and impact across Africa and beyond.

Over the past decade, we have done the work of building; building credibility, building community, and building capability. It has taken persistence, belief, and the commitment of many people who chose to invest their time, energy, and trust in this work. Along the way, we’ve learned what drives real, lasting impact in leadership. As a result, we enter 2026 with a clear understanding of who we are, what we do best, and how we intend to continue growing.

Our theme for the year is 10 Years. 10X Impact. Not as a celebration of scale for its own sake, but as a marker of how far we’ve come, and the responsibility that comes with it. This is a year of momentum built on clarity.

In 2026, we are focused on three things that matter most:


  • Becoming an even stronger search partner to the multinationals, DFIs, investors, early-stage companies, and impact organisations shaping the future, with Executive Search firmly at the centre of our work
  • Deepening the strength and readiness of our community, ensuring executive talent is visible, trusted, and connected to the right opportunities
  • Putting our technology to work, using it to sharpen insight, improve effectiveness, and scale impact without losing quality

Ten years in, our work feels more focused than ever. What has changed is not the ambition, but the confidence that comes from experience. We know what matters, and we are committed to building with intention, operating with discipline, and delivering impact that compounds over time.

To our members, partners, clients, and wider community, thank you. This milestone belongs to you as much as it does to TheBoardroom Africa. Your trust, belief, and partnership have shaped what this organisation has become.

Here’s to 2026, and to the many more decades ahead.

Warm regards,

CEO
