Session Recap: The Role of Finance in Driving Strategy

2025 - Session 1

Session Takeaways


Thank you for actively participating in the Segment 2 - The Role of Finance in Driving Strategy sessions with Talita Ferreira.

In this session, we explored how financial insight is essential for strategic leadership and sustainable value creation at board level. We began by examining the powerful intersection between strategy and finance, highlighting how directors must balance short-term performance with long-term investments and risk awareness.

The session deepened with practical tools for financial board leadership, ranging from investment appraisal techniques and value driver frameworks to effective use of KPIs and capital efficiency strategies. We also addressed key financial market value drivers, including ESG and intangibles, and their impact on company valuation.

As the session progressed, we unpacked how to build financial roadmaps through forecasting, scenario planning, and strategic resource allocation.

We closed by exploring how holistic thinking, effective risk oversight, and board-level financial intelligence are essential to delivering strategy and navigating uncertainty.

Key Takeaways:

  • Finance Is Central to Strategic Decision-Making - Boards must balance performance, risk, and long-term value.
  • Directors Need to Think Like Investors - Ask: Is this the best use of capital?
  • Value Creation Goes Beyond Financial Statements - ESG, brand, and culture also shape market value.
  • KPIs and Financial Metrics Must Be Aligned With Strategy - Use KPIs that reflect strategy, not just performance.
  • Forecasting and Scenario Planning Strengthen Board Foresight - Forecasting and scenarios help boards stay ahead.
