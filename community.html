In Memory

We were deeply saddened about the loss of Cynthia Quarcoo, a distinguished Legal Practitioner and valued member of the TBrA community. Her legal prowess was matched only by her compassion, as she tirelessly advocated for those who needed a voice. Cynthia’s legacy lives through her successes, including the establishment of Ghana's first Entertainment, Sports & Media practice. Our deepest condolences go out to her family, friends, and colleagues during this challenging period.
