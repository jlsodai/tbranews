News



We're pleased to share that TheBoardroom Africa (TBrA) has taken a bold step toward our mission to foster diversity within the corporate landscape by expanding our membership to include male candidates.

This move reflects our conviction that our efforts to enhance leadership opportunities for women and create diverse leadership spaces must be anchored in the curation of a diverse network and community. Furthermore, in order to drive sustainable change, it is critical that leaders, regardless of their gender, have access to the right tools and insights to engage in meaningful discourse to drive impactful change.

We embark on this new chapter and continue to innovate new ways of driving impactful change. We're excited about the possibilities it will bring and remain profoundly grateful to our TBrA community for your unwavering support of our work.

Diversity for Impact at IFC's Annual
Nominee Director Forum


On 11 June, Marcia Ashong-Sam, CEO TheBoardroom Africa, and TBrA member, Malado Kaba, Board Director and former Minister of Economy & Finance, Guinea, participated in a compelling panel discussion on ‘Diversity for Impact’ at the IFC's Annual Nominee Director Forum in Washington, DC. Joining them were Jennifer Reynolds, CEO of Women Corporate Directors and Patricia Rodriguez Christian, Co-Chair of Women Executives on Boards. The panel delved into insightful research findings on diversity's role in fostering impact; the significant shortcomings in current diversity practices and strategies to enhance inclusivity and representation at the highest levels of corporate governance.
