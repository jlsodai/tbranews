In Memory
Suzanne Beigel


We are deeply saddened by the loss of Suzanne Beigel, a remarkable impact investor, philanthropist, and dedicated adviser known for championing gender-lens investing.

Suzanne's indomitable spirit and vision have left an indelible mark on the world of finance and social impact, and her legacy will forever resonate through her groundbreaking work, inspiring countless individuals to follow in her footsteps.

Our deepest condolences go out to Suzanne Beigel's family, friends, and colleagues during this difficult time; her impact on the world will never be forgotten, and her legacy will continue to shine brightly in our hearts and in the work we do to create a more equitable and inclusive future.
