Open Doors — Cohort 15

Session Takeaways

Thank you for your active participation during our first module on the Role of the Director and the Board.


Janhavi gave us a practical understanding and applicable strategies to bring best practices to the boardroom. We explored the board’s role in corporate governance and in navigating the role of stakeholder management while acting in the organisation’s best interests. To that effect, we looked at the director’s key duties, roles, and legal responsibilities and identified the differences between direction, management and ownership in driving corporate governance towards improving the board’s effectiveness.


Key Takeaways:


  • Mitigating liability
  • The Directors Duties & Board Performance Review
  • Risk Governance
  • Ethics and Culture
