GHADA HAMMOUDA

Group Chief Sustainability & Marketing Officer, Qalaa Holdings


Ghada Hammouda is the Chief Sustainability & Marketing Officer at Qalaa Holdings, leading significant public listings and expansions across Africa. With over 30 years at companies like IBM, AT&T, and Ogilvy & Mather, she excels in ESG strategy, branding, and market development. Recognised among Egypt’s Top 50 Women in Business and as a Forbes top marketing professional, Ghada has won numerous awards, including the David Ogilvy Award. A passionate advocate for sustainability and gender equality, she frequently speaks on these topics and has served on several boards including the UN Global Compact Local Network in Egypt. Ghada also mentors at the Egyptian Banking Institute, shaping future leadership.



CONNECT

TSHEPISO KOBILE

Chief Executive Officer, Southern African Venture Capital & Private Equity Association


Tshepiso Kobile is a seasoned professional in property and infrastructure finance. Her expertise spans transaction structuring, due diligence, and infrastructure programme management. Tshepiso actively serves on industry bodies and multi-stakeholder councils, advocating for inclusive growth, spatial transformation, private – public sector partnerships and sustainable infrastructure investment. Currently, she is the CEO of the Southern African Venture Capital and Private Equity Association (an Association servicing Members with collective AUM of R237bn), Non-Executive Director and member of the Credit and Social & Ethics Committees of the National Housing Finance Corporation (a housing finance entity that is being transitioned into the Human Settlement Development Bank).

JAMES KONI

Co-Founder and Executive Director, BestLife Insurance Limited


James Koni is an accomplished Zambian C-suite banking and finance executive with 26 years of experience across finance and banking in Africa and Europe. He has successfully led business transformations, modernisation, and strategic turnarounds, enhancing governance and shareholder value. James spearheaded a significant banking merger and digital transformation, introducing a global award-winning mobile wallet. He has built and led high-performing teams, maintaining strong relationships with boards, shareholders, and strategic partners such as the Trade and Investment Bank (TDB). Recognised as a banking industry leader, James is passionate about impactful delivery.





CONNECT

NDIARKA MBODJI

Founder & CEO, Kowry Energy


Ndiarka Mbodji, Founder & CEO of Kowry Energy Services, is a seasoned transformation leader operating at Main Board and Executive Leadership level, with some non-executive Director experience across Energy, Aerospace, Automotive, and other industries. As an ex-Rolls-Royce executive, she excels in strategic thinking, delivering change with a focus on quality and profitability. She has a proven track record of navigating complex global environments, achieving operational and financial success through team coaching and international partnerships. A French national with Senegalese heritage, Ndiarka holds a degree in Chemistry and is a graduate of Dartmouth Business School.



CONNECT

BABACAR SECK

Managing Partner, ASKYA Investment Partners


Babacar Seck is a Managing Partner at Askya Investment Partners, passionate about fostering African development through entrepreneurship. He has supported and invested in leading African startups such as Charged, Jumia, GoMyCode, Jetstream, and Moneyfellows. Previously, Babacar was CEO of Digital Africa, driving its transformation into a platform for African startups, and a founding member of Proparco's €300m Venture Capital programme. At Proparco, he also led financial services private equity, supporting landmark investments and exits across banking and insurance, and served as a board member of multinationals, funds and startups. With a background at AXA, Lazard, and the OECD, Babacar holds degrees from Sciences Po, Tsinghua, and Renmin University.
