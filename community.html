Unveiling Our New Digital Home: Explore TBrA's New Website


We’re excited to unveil TheBoardroom Africa’s brand-new website!

The refreshed platform offers a user-friendly experience with an elevated look, designed to make it easier to explore our services, and connect with top leadership talent. The website’s intuitive interface allows for seamless access to everything from our curated leadership search solutions, to executive education programmes and our new innovative platform - TExEd.

The site also introduces our upcoming AI-driven search solution, which will transform how leaders are placed in board and executive roles.

Whether you're looking to grow your career or diversify your board talent, our new website has everything you need at your fingertips.

By 2023, 85% of corporate directors were independent, a trend shaped by post-Watergate distrust and regulatory changes. However, studies reveal no strong link between board independence and performance. Inside directors offer key advantages, including deep company knowledge, a focus on long-term success, and a balanced board dynamic, prompting reconsideration of their value.
