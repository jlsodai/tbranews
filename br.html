Inkomoko

Independent Non-Executive Directors


Industry: Microfinance

Inkomoko, founded in 2012, is a non-profit organisation driving business growth in Africa. Currently serving 33,000 entrepreneurs annually in Rwanda, Kenya, Ethiopia, and South Sudan, Inkomoko focuses on marginalised groups like refugees, youth, and women. With a team of 400, the organisation provides training, consulting, and access to finance, boasting the status of Africa's largest lender to displaced entrepreneurs. In 2024, Inkomoko plans a $33 million USD budget, allocating $17 million to micro-loans across Africa.

Inkomoko is searching for four (4) Independent Non-Executive Directors to join their board as replacements, one (1) of whom will assume the Chair role.

The application deadline for this role is 24 March at midnight, 2024.

AXIAN Energy

Non-Executive Director


Industry: Energy

Founded in Madagascar 150 years ago, Axian Group is a leading investor in Africa, focusing on Real Estate, Telecoms, Financial Services, Energy, and Open Innovation & Fintech. Axian Energy, a vital part of the group, provides innovative energy solutions across the continent, prioritising accessibility and sustainability. From construction to operation, Axian Energy covers the entire energy value chain, specialising in renewable energies and the promotion of energy inclusion in Africa.

Axian Energy is searching for one (1) Non-Executive Director to join their board as well as the Audit & Risk and Governance committees.

The application deadline for this role is 13 March at midnight, 2024.
