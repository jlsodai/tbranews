A transformational three-part series designed to deepen self-awareness and develop the foundations and strengthen the core capabilities of effective leadership.



Topics

Session 1: The Power of Values in Leadership

This session connects participants to the deep influence of their values, the beliefs that quietly shape decisions, relationships, and impact.

You will examine both the empowering and limiting aspects of your values, uncovering the stories that shape them and recognising where outdated beliefs may be holding you back. Through guided reflection, you will learn to interrogate and recalibrate your values, aligning with what is authentic and relevant for who you are today. This session equips you with the clarity and self-awareness needed to lead with greater conviction, integrity, and personal power.

Session 2: Transitioning into Management, Powerful Presence

This session grounds participants in the realities of stepping into management‚Äîa transition that often happens without formal preparation but carries significant organisational and personal implications.

You will explore the core shifts required to move from contributor to manager, gaining insight into the mindsets, responsibilities, and behaviours that define effective leadership at this level. Through practical frameworks and reflective exercises, you will learn how to build trust, provide clarity, and support the performance of your team. This session equips you with the tools, confidence, and awareness needed to navigate your leadership transition with competence, empathy, and credibility.

Session 3: Anticipating and Managing Conflict

This session helps participants understand the integral role of conflict in leadership, reframing it from something to fear into a powerful opportunity for clarity, learning, and growth.

You will explore the theoretical foundations of workplace conflict while examining your own relationship with tension, discomfort, and difficult conversations. Through guided reflection and practice, you will identify common pitfalls in conflict situations and learn a structured approach for navigating and resolving disagreements. This session equips you with the confidence, emotional steadiness, and practical tools needed to engage with conflict constructively and lead with greater courage and intention.
