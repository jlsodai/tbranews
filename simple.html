EMERGE Programme Overview

TheBoardroom Africa’s EMERGE (Empowering Minds to Embrace Resilience, Growth, and Excellence) is a holistic leadership development programme where participants aged 25 - 35 will benefit from a range of learning and development opportunities, including mentorship and coaching, as well as a range of workshops and webinars. Our goal is to equip these emerging African leaders with the tools they need to excel in their careers; cultivating the next generation of leaders who are catalysts for positive change across the continent.

Requirements

The mentorship engagement will include six (6) one-hour sessions over a six-month period between June 2024 and January 2025. These sessions may include discussions around:

  • Career Guidance: Providing crucial insights that support their professional and personal development;
  • Leadership Skills Enhancement: Building their capacity to lead with authenticity and ethical integrity,
  • Navigating Challenges: Helping them set and achieve significant goals;
  • Exploration in a Safe Space: Encouraging free thought and innovation within a nurturing environment.

Technology and Support

Mentor sessions will be conducted virtually, with mentors receiving assistance from the TBrA Team to set up Calendly links as needed for scheduling sessions with their mentees.

The Commitment

Mentors are expected to:

  • Leverage their extensive leadership experience to guide their mentee(s) to fulfil their full potential;
  • Foster a deep connection with their mentee(s) by cultivating an empowering, trust-based relationships;
  • Encourage their mentees' unique perspectives to enrich the EMERGE community's collective strength.

EMERGE represents our commitment to nurturing the next generation of African leadership. As a mentor, your expertise and experience will greatly benefit and contribute to shaping our future leaders. To learn more, visit the EMERGE website .
