An unforgettable evening at the Nairobi City Mixer

We are pleased to share the success of our recent Nairobi City Mixer. With over a 100 guests in attendance, the evening was filled with vibrant discussions, inspiring connections, and a shared commitment to driving diversity in boardrooms. Guests had the opportunity to network and exchange ideas with like minded executives and some of the most influential leaders in Kenya’s business community.

Thank you to everyone who participated and made our first in-person event in Nairobi such a memorable occasion. We look forward to more enriching interactions in the future.
