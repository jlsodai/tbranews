Message from the CEO

As we conclude 2024, I reflect on a year defined by bold decisions, transformative progress, and the enduring strength of our community. This year’s theme, Breaking Barriers, Building Bridges, has guided us through pivotal milestones, advancing our mission to reshape corporate leadership across Africa and beyond.

Expanding Our Community for Greater Impact

One of our most significant strides this year was opening TheBoardroom Africa’s membership to male leaders—a bold yet necessary step. True progress in leadership requires collaboration across genders, industries, and geographies. By broadening our community, we strengthened our commitment to inclusion while expanding our capacity to drive meaningful change.

Our male members are embracing the spirit of diversity and shared responsibility, enriching our conversations with fresh perspectives and reinforcing our resolve to break barriers in leadership representation. Together, we are creating a space where diverse voices converge to shape the future of corporate governance.

Driving Thought Leadership and Advocacy

This year, we deepened our influence through powerful collaborations and strategic initiatives. The 5th Ghana Board Diversity Index Report and the 4th Egypt Board Diversity Index Report were published this year, offering actionable insights and driving change in boardroom representation across Africa.

We were honoured to be the spotlight of a case study published by British International Investment (BII), titled "TheBoardroom Africa: Increasing Women Leaders in Africa." This in-depth feature highlighted the transformative impact of our early partnership with BII, which laid the foundation for our growth and success. BII's recognition and ongoing support underscore the significant strides we have made in advancing diversity in leadership spaces across Africa, and the case study serves as a powerful testament to the value of investing in inclusive leadership.

In addition, TheBoardroom Africa was invited to contribute to BII’s comprehensive research study, "Catalysing the Field of Impact Investing: How Field-Building Organisations Make and Measure Their Efforts." This study places TheBoardroom Africa among some of the leading players in the impact investing ecosystem and explores the innovative approaches driving meaningful change.

We were also featured in the June edition of the Chartered Accountants Institute magazine, which highlighted our transformative impact on leadership spaces through our distinctive focus on governance and sustainability. The article underscored the increasing recognition of TheBoardroom Africa’s vital role in redefining modern leadership practices and advancing equity in boardrooms and executive spaces.

Our participation in global forums, such as the IFC’s Annual Nominee Director Forum in Washington, DC, amplified our advocacy on a global stage, ensuring Africa’s evolving leadership narrative remains central to international conversations about progress and equity.

Expanding Access to Executive Education

Demand for executive education tailored to Africa’s unique leadership challenges surged in 2024. Record enrolments in our flagship Board Certificate and ESG Certificate Programmes reflected the growing appetite for leadership development with a global lens and regional context.

The launch of TExED, our new Learning Management System, and the redesigned Certificate in Modern Board Direction (CMBD) underscored our commitment to delivering cutting-edge, impactful learning experiences steeped in the complex and ever-changing landscape of leadership.

Strengthening Community and Connection

Our work was enriched by moments of deep connection—whether through our City Mixers in Nairobi or partnerships with entities like the Ghana Stock Exchange. These initiatives brought our community closer, enabling cross-border knowledge-sharing and fostering relationships built on shared purpose.

Looking Ahead

While we celebrate our progress, we also acknowledge that growth comes with challenges. I am deeply grateful for the dedication, resilience, and passion of the entire TBrA team. Your hard work fuels our success and strengthens our impact every day.

To our members, partners, and supporters—thank you for believing in our mission and for sharing your expertise, vision, and commitment to shaping a more inclusive world. You are the heart of TBrA and the driving force behind everything we accomplish.

Looking ahead to 2025, I’m thrilled about our plans to further leverage technology to expand access to leadership opportunities and development. Our commitment to delivering top, diverse leadership talent at scale remains stronger than ever as we equip leaders with the tools they need to drive transformative change in the businesses they lead.

Wishing you a joyous festive season and a prosperous New Year.

Warm regards,

What's upcoming next year

The expansion of our TExED Learning Management System (LMS) will deliver a seamless and immersive experience for busy executives. The platform will host a wide range of resources, including courses, webinars, and interactive modules, accessible anytime, anywhere. Features like personalised learning paths and real-time progress tracking will empower executives to upskill efficiently, staying ahead in an ever-evolving business landscape.

Set to launch in 2025, our new technology platform, backed by advanced AI, will combine leadership acquisition and leadership development in a way that revolutionises how talent is sourced and developed. We’re excited to share updates on our beta testing and will share updates on our official launch next year.
