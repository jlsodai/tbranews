ESG IN PRACTICE



The aviation industry faces increasing pressure to manage costs while meeting stringent environmental standards. Lufthansa Group, one of the largest airline groups globally, recognised the need for better visibility into its procurement spend and carbon footprint. With data spread across 14 separate ERP applications, the company sought to gain more transparency and actionable insights.

In 2022, Lufthansa partnered with McKinsey to develop Spendscape, a SaaS solution integrated with SAP’s Business Technology Platform (BTP). This solution aimed to create a comprehensive view of procurement spend, transforming siloed data streams into a unified, global perspective, enhancing both cost management and sustainability efforts.

Impact:


  • Enhanced Spend Transparency: Spendscape provides Lufthansa with a detailed, 360-degree view of procurement performance, first implemented at Lufthansa Technik and now being rolled out across the group.
  • Informed Decision Making: The tool enables smarter purchasing decisions by integrating near-real-time data on price volatility and carbon emissions, alongside traditional spend data.
  • Operational Improvements: The solution simplifies data integration processes, informs supplier negotiations, and supports near-real-time cash position management.
  • Carbon Footprint Insights: Spendscape unites procurement data from over ten ERP systems, creating a searchable source of truth that includes detailed carbon footprint information.

Key Learnings:


  • Data Integration is Crucial: A harmonised data platform is essential for comprehensive spend visibility and effective decision-making.
  • Sustainability and Savings: Integrating sustainability metrics with spend data helps in identifying cost-saving opportunities while meeting environmental goals.
  • Collaboration and Transparency: Engaging stakeholders and maintaining transparency are vital for successful implementation and adoption.
