Annabel Tagoe-Bannerman

Group General Counsel, Bakkavor Group Plc


Annabel is an experienced senior executive within UK-listed companies across the retail, leisure, food and beverage sectors. An alumnus of the London Business School, she is a qualified corporate and commercial solicitor. Annabel is currently Group General Counsel and Company Secretary of Bakkavor Group plc, the FTSE 250 listed producer of freshly prepared food, where she also chairs the Diversity & Inclusion Forum. Annabel is also a Non-Directive of Edinburgh Investment Trust Plc.

Rachel Moré-Oshodi

Deputy VP Commercial Sales, Africa, TotalEnergies

TBrA Certificates: Open Doors

Rachel Moré-Oshodi is an accomplished executive committed to building Africa’s energy sector. She has extensive global and local expertise in developing, executing, and managing projects across multiple sectors and regions. Her partnerships with energy companies, financial institutions & DFIs, private equity funds, blue chip corporations, and start-ups have delivered over US$ 7 billion worth of pioneering, award-winning projects, which have helped shape the landscape of project finance in Africa and advanced UN Sustainable Development Goals.

Zoë Karl-Waithaka

Managing Director & Partner, Boston Consulting Group

TBrA Certificates: Open Doors

Zoë Karl-Waithaka is a South African-American management consultant with experience advising public, private and social sector clients across the African continent. She is passionate about growing African businesses and economies to achieve their full potential and has led successful projects in multiple countries across the globe. Her work includes the development of growth strategies, building inclusive markets, designing and delivering large-scale transformation programs, and working with people inside organisations to increase impact from change efforts.

Tumi Chamayou

Group Executive: Enterprise Business Unit, MTN Group


Tumi is a seasoned ICT professional with over 15 years of experience in Telecoms, Technology and Management Consulting. She joined MTN Group as a General Manager for Large Enterprise sales in 2018 and has played a pivotal role in driving enterprise sales to achieve MTN’s Ambition 2025 targets. Tumi currently is the Group Executive for Enterprise Business Unit and oversees the strategic and operational alignment of various teams across 18 countries. Previously she held various senior leadership roles at Ericsson, Egon Zehnder, Accenture and Decipher.
