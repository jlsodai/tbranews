Message from the CEO

Reflecting on 2023: A Year of Growth and Empowerment


As we bid farewell to 2023, we reflect on a year marked by growth, resilience, and remarkable achievements at TheBoardroom Africa. This year has been a testament to the power and impact of our dynamic network of women leaders, who have persevered and thrived across diverse industries in Africa and the diaspora.

Our network has seen substantial growth, now encompassing more than 2,700 women representing over 70 nationalities and sectors. Each of you has contributed to our shared mission to transform the face of the boardroom, enhancing the collective strength and diversity of our community. We have proudly supported the growth and placement of over 30 women in pivotal roles, including board directors, executive directors and trustees, augmenting the depth and breadth of expertise within our ranks.

This year marked the deepening and expansion of our executive development programmes. From our flagship Open Doors Programme to our successful Environmental, Social & Governance (ESG) Certificate Programme, we have continued to pave the way for excellence in governance and leadership. In partnership with The DCRO Risk Governance Institute, we launched two new Risk Governance programmes, further diversifying our educational offerings. We celebrated a significant milestone in our Open Doors programme, having welcomed over 200 delegates to date, and were delighted to resume in-person sessions this November in Cape Town. This gathering was a special occasion to recognise our alumni who are on their transformative journey to become Chartered Directors.

In our continuous effort to enhance your membership experience, we introduced several new services and programmes. Our City Mixers in Accra and Lagos brought together an impressive number of members for enriching interactions and networking opportunities. Plans are underway to extend these events to additional cities, with the invaluable support of our dedicated TBrA Ambassadors. We also launched WISE Circles, a forum designed for collective problem-solving and peer networking, and introduced a Thought Leadership Positioning Offering to further empower our members.

Our commitment to promoting governance best practices remained steadfast, as evidenced by the launch of the Free Governance Baseline Tool. This innovative tool provides organisations with in-depth analysis and actionable insights, fostering the harnessing of gender diversity's power. Our advocacy for transparency and progress in board diversity continued with the publication of new editions of our Board Diversity Index report, in partnership with local stock exchanges in each respective country.

In the words of the late Suzanne Biegel, a friend of TBrA and champion of our community, “If you’re not waking up every day and thinking about your part of the solution, then I don’t know what you’re doing.” As we step into the new year, we honour the legacy of Suzanne by strengthening our resolve to elevate women leaders and foster inclusive leadership cultures through our efforts.

As we step into the new year, our resolve to elevate women leaders and foster inclusive leadership cultures is stronger than ever. We extend our heartfelt gratitude to each of you for being an integral part of this journey and for entrusting us with your growth and aspirations.

Wishing you a joyous festive season and a prosperous New Year.

Warm regards,

Member Spotlight

Every year we spotlight the extraordinary achievements of some of our women leaders. This year we feature Nathalie Akon Gabala and Tariye Gbadegesi.



Nathalie Akon Gabala


Nathalie Akon Gabala assumed the role of Director of the Gender & Economic Inclusion Department at IFC in September 2023, transitioning from her position as Executive Director at Mastercard Foundation. With extensive leadership experience, Nathalie previously directed the Foundation's Pan-Africa platform, overseeing thematic areas such as Gender, Entrepreneurship Development, Digital Economy, and Agrifood Systems and Climate. Earlier in her career, she served as Regional Director, expanding the Foundation's presence in West, Central, and Northern Africa.

Nathalie has a background as a Partner & Managing Director at Equator Capital Partners, an impact investment fund manager, and has held influential roles in African banks. Recognised in the 2022 list of the 100 most influential women by Advance Media, Nathalie holds a Master of Management in Strategy and a Minor in Finance from Ecole Supérieure de Commerce de Paris, France.





Tariye Gbadegesi


In November 2023, Tariye Gbadegesi was appointed as the CEO of the Climate Investment Funds. A seasoned investment professional with over two decades of experience, Tariye has successfully led investments exceeding $3 billion in large-scale energy, infrastructure, and industrial projects. Presently the Managing Director and CEO of ARM Harith Infrastructure Fund Managers (Armhif), she manages a joint venture between Asset & Resource Management Company Ltd (ARM) and Harith General Partners (Pty) Ltd (Harith).

Tariye played a crucial role in establishing the Africa Finance Corporation (AFC), a Pan-African DFI managing US$6 billion, where she led multiple investments in power, transport, industrial, and telecommunications infrastructure sectors. Her extensive board experience includes contributions to notable projects like Amandi IPP in Ghana and Main One Cable Company, a Pan-African sub-sea and ICT company. She has also served on the board of Cabeolica S.A, a Cabo Verdean wind power IPP, recognised as a top 10 PPP project in sub-Saharan Africa by IFC.

NEW for 2024: Risk Governance Programmes


By understanding and proactively managing risks, organisations can mitigate potential pitfalls and seize opportunities that may arise in an ever-changing global landscape. We are excited to announce our partnership with the US-based DCRO Risk Governance Institute offering the TBrA community special access to the two flagship programmes on risk governance: The Board Members Course on Risk and The Certificate in Cyber Risk Governance.

Programmes

Certificate in Risk Governance® - The Board Members’ Course on Risk®


Elevate your board leadership with the Board Members’ Course on Risk®. This self-paced programme empowers current and aspiring board members with comprehensive risk governance skills.
