The Environmental Social and Governance (ESG) Certificate returns for an additional two cohorts in 2024. The programme targets women directors and executives looking to develop their understanding of ESG issues and concepts, equipping them with the tools required to drive ESG agendas in their respective organisations. The expert-led curriculum incorporates ESG theoretical frameworks and trends within a global context, with practical inputs intended to support executives as they review their roles in driving the ESG agenda.

The 2024 programme is enriched with guest speakers from a variety of sectors who will lean on the practical application of ESG as executives, board members, and sustainability leaders.
