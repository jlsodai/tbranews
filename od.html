What to Expect

The Certificate is made up of four modules:

  • Role of the Director and the Board: Covers the responsibilities and duties of directors and the board, governance structures, and the role of the chairman.
  • Finance for Non-Finance Directors: Provides a comprehensive understanding of financial terms and concepts, how to interpret financial statements, and the financial duties of directors.
  • Strategy for Directors: Focuses on strategic thinking, the development and implementation of strategic plans, and the director's role in shaping and guiding strategy.
  • Leadership for Directors: Explores leadership styles and their impact, leading through change, and the director’s role in building effective teams and fostering a positive corporate culture.

The sessions include interactive workshops, board simulation, case studies, and practical exercises, which are integral parts of the learning process.
