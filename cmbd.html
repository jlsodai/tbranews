What makes CMBD different from other governance programmes?

CMBD is tailored for senior leaders navigating modern boardroom realities. It’s grounded in global standards and designed to reflect the complexity and context of governance on the continent, offering both regional insight and global perspective.

I already serve on a board, what value does this programme add?

CMBD helps experienced board members sharpen their strategic lens, understand evolving governance trends, and deepen their influence through peer exchange and global faculty insight.

I’m not on a board yet. Is this programme right for me?

Yes, CMBD equips aspiring directors with the knowledge, tools, and credibility to be board-ready and effectively contribute from day one.

How time-intensive is the programme?

The programme is designed with executive schedules in mind. It blends live sessions and self-paced modules, allowing flexibility without compromising depth.

What outcomes can I expect?

You’ll gain a recognised credential, a sharpened governance skillset, access to an exclusive network, and greater confidence in navigating strategic board responsibilities.
