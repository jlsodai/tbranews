Spearhead resilience on every board with Open Doors


Open Doors is a highly regarded director certificate programme, developed in collaboration with the prestigious Institute of Directors UK (IoD UK).

This programme aims to equip participants with the necessary knowledge and foresight to successfully navigate the boardroom. With its global recognition and accreditation, Open Doors offers an empowering experience for individuals seeking to enhance their directorial abilities.

The curriculum includes four core modules focused on leadership, strategy, finance, and governance, accompanied by additional workshops on board dynamics and ontological leadership.

NEW: Risk Governance Programmes


Designed and delivered in partnership with the Institute of Directors UK (IoD), Open Doors is a globally recognised and accredited director certificate programme suited to existing and aspiring directors. The programme provides a comprehensive curriculum covering the essential components of leadership, strategy, finance and governance and offers additional insights into board dynamics and ontological leadership through curated workshops.

The Board Members' Course on Risk®


The Board Members’ Course on Risk® is a comprehensive self-paced board development programme that aims to enhance focus on the positive and effective governance of risk-taking by boards of directors. The programme is designed for current and aspiring board members and is led by over 50 board members, executives, and authors. Upon completion, graduates are awarded the Certificate in Risk Governance®.



Certificate in Cyber Risk Governance


The Certificate in Cyber Risk Governance credential is designed for either current or aspiring board members who wish to effectively oversee cyber risks and the use of technologies as part of their fiduciary duties. The course material includes lectures and case study interviews with prominent directors, c-level executives, law enforcement, intelligence, and technology experts, providing essential practical knowledge to help you become more effective in boardroom strategic planning and oversight.
