NEW for 2024: Risk Governance Programmes


By understanding and proactively managing risks, organisations can mitigate potential pitfalls and seize opportunities that may arise in an ever-changing global landscape. We are excited to announce our partnership with the US-based DCRO Risk Governance Institute offering the TBrA community special access to the two flagship programmes on risk governance: The Board Members Course on Risk and The Certificate in Cyber Risk Governance.

Risk Governance
Programmes

Certificate in Risk Governance® - The Board Members’ Course on Risk®

Elevate your board leadership with the Board Members’ Course on Risk®. This self-paced programme empowers current and aspiring board members with comprehensive risk governance skills. Led by 50+ experienced board members, executives, and authors, it awards graduates the esteemed Certificate in Risk Governance® upon completion.



Certificate in Cyber Risk Governance

Tailored for current or aspiring board members, this programme empowers participants to effectively oversee cyber risks and technology as part of their fiduciary duties. Learn from prominent directors, C-level executives, and experts through lectures and case studies designed to enhance strategic oversight skills in the boardroom.
