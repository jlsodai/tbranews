Hear from Rosalind Kainyah



“The TBrA ESG certificate programme will address the tensions between financial performance and ESG practices and equip and empower African business leaders to find the right and appropriate solutions to their specific sustainability challenges.”

There is growing emphasis on sustainability in businesses worldwide. Integrating Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) practices into core business operations has become crucial for companies looking to secure long-term success and maintain stakeholder trust.

In the context of African businesses facing challenges in balancing financial performance with ESG practices, it's important to highlight that sustainable business practices can actually drive long-term value creation.

While there may be initial costs associated with implementing ESG initiatives, the benefits in terms of risk mitigation, brand reputation, stakeholder engagement, and operational efficiency can outweigh these costs in the long run.

The TBrA ESG certificate programme will address the tensions between financial performance and ESG practices and equip and empower African business leaders to find the right and appropriate solutions to their specific sustainability challenges. These solutions can contribute to and protect the value of their enterprises. By adopting a strategic and proactive approach to integrating ESG practices into their operations, African businesses can not only enhance their long-term resilience and competitiveness but also contribute to sustainable development in the region.
