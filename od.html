About Open Doors

Open Doors offers a curriculum that combines technical analysis with practical applications for effective board directorship. The programme is ideal for women leaders looking to enhance their strategic governance skills and board-level expertise.

Beyond the curriculum, each cohort also offers an enriched networking experience to delegates from diverse industries and nationalities.
