[[trackingImage]] ESG Certificate

95% of all leadership and investment decisions will be shaped by ESG strategy by 2035. Are you ready for that future? Join this 5-week certificate programme to understand what it takes to adapt, transform, and authentically lead in our new global context with ESG awareness.



About the Programme


Led by a faculty of experts, the certificate programme is delivered over 12 sessions between February and March 2023. Here are some key learning highlights:


Explore the history of ESG and the roles of different actors in the ecosystem

Master the development of an ESG action plan

Recognise the commercial, reputational and societal returns of ESG

Learn how to manage ESG risks and how to create effective ESG systems

Understand how to design an effective system for ESG oversight

Utilise frameworks and best practices to avoid greenwashing
