CEO's New Year Message

Happy New Year to you all,

As we step into 2025, we embark on an exciting chapter for TheBoardroom Africa, one that builds upon the strong foundation we have laid over the years. This is a defining moment in our journey toward transforming how leadership talent is acquired, nurtured, and empowered across Africa and beyond.

2024 was a pivotal year for us. We expanded our community and services and embraced technology to deepen our impact. We are grateful to our partners, members, and clients — whose support and collaboration have driven our growth.

In 2025, we are stepping into our boldest phase yet. Guided by our refined purpose — to build a future of sustainable business excellence led by the most diverse and talented leaders — we will:

  • Expand our leadership talent acquisition services to support businesses and organisations of all sizes, leveraging data and technology to deliver seamless, impactful placements that drive organisations toward their strategic objectives.
  • Build on our community-focused approach, fostering stronger relationships with Africa's top leadership talent and connecting them with transformative opportunities and professional support.
  • Launch the beta version of our AI-driven platform, designed to revolutionise how leadership talent is identified, nurtured, and connected to opportunities.
  • Transform the delivery of our executive education programmes, integrating technology to expand our reach and adapt to the evolving needs of modern leaders.
  • Introduce our revamped Certificate in Modern Board Direction, incorporating key elements like sustainability and digital transformation to equip leaders for today's complex governance challenges.
  • Continue to deliver and enhance the ESG Certificate Programme, solidifying its position as the gold standard in sustainability training.
  • Develop new events and masterclasses in collaboration with global experts, ensuring our offerings remain relevant and impactful.

To achieve these ambitions, we are also expanding our team to bring in the expertise required to lead these business units and drive our growth trajectory.

2025 represents our boldest phase yet — a year to consolidate our growth, to be transformative, and to showcase the potential of what we've built.

Here's to a year of bold moves and collective success.

Warm regards,

Founder & CEO
