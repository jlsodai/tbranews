The African Risk Capacity Insurance Company Limited

Non-Executive Directors


Industry: Financial Services

The African Risk Capacity Insurance Company Limited (ARC Ltd) is a financial arm of the African Risk Capacity (ARC), a specialised agency of the African Union (AU), with a mission to assist African governments in mitigating the financial impacts of natural disasters. Supported by US$100 million in funding from UK FCDO and Germany BMZ via KFW, ARC Ltd is a mutual insurance company owned by African sovereigns. Through insurance coverage and prompt claims payment, ARC Ltd operates as a groundbreaking public-private partnership to enhance economic growth in Africa and empower governments to safeguard their citizens.

ARC Ltd is searching for 2 (two) Non-Executive Directors representing member countries to join their board as replacements for retiring directors.

The application deadline for this role is 13 October at midnight, 2023.

Aceli Africa

Non-Executive Directors


Industry: Agricultural Finance

Aceli, established in 2020, is a nonprofit market incentive facility promoting agricultural small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) lending. Partnering with banks and investors, they've mobilised $125M in loans for 1,200 SMEs, impacting 750,000 smallholder farmers across Kenya, Rwanda, Tanzania, and Uganda. Aceli now plans to expand its operations to Zambia. Their ambitious goal is to raise $1.2B by 2028, aiming to uplift the livelihoods of 2 million farmers.

Aceli Africa is searching for 2 (two) Non-Executive Directors to join their global board.

The application deadline for this role is 6 September at midnight, 2023.

International Finance Corporation

Non-Executive Director


Industry: Financial Services

International Finance Corporation (IFC), a member of the World Bank Group, is the largest global development institution focused on the private sector in developing countries. They aim to end extreme poverty and promote shared prosperity by 2030. IFC provides investment, advisory, and asset management services to encourage private-sector development in less developed countries.

IFC is seeking to nominate 1 (one) Non-Executive Director to the board of a financial institution (housing finance) operating in Tanzania.

The application deadline for this role is 31 August at midnight, 2023.

The Fred Hollows Foundation Kenya

Non-Executive Directors


Industry: Non-Profit

The Fred Hollows Foundation Kenya is part of a global group, with a presence in 25 countries worldwide, dedicated to ending avoidable blindness and treating vision impairment. They ensure equitable access to high-quality eye care services for all Kenyans, prioritising advocacy and collaboration with governments and NGOs. With a comprehensive approach and a focus on sustainability, they strive to prevent blindness and restore sight. Their global governance roadmap empowers local directors for meaningful impact at the local level.

The Fred Hollows Foundation Kenya is searching for 2 (two) Non-Executive Directors to join their board.

The application deadline for this role is 18 August at midnight, 2023.
