Module Details

We look forward to seeing you at the upcoming Governance module, Role of the Director and the Board, with Janhavi Dadarkar on Thursday, 25 July and Friday, 26 July 2024. Both day's sessions run from 08:00 AM - 16:00 PM GMT.


In these sessions, you will be introduced to the director's duties and legal responsibilities and the board's role in corporate governance and risk management. These sessions will help you gain a better understanding of best practices for directors, identify your role as a board director in the context of your operating environment and ways to manage stakeholder relationships while acting in the company or organisation's best interest.
