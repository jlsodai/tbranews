UpEnergy

Independent Non-Executive Director


Industry: Clean Technology

Rooted in rigorous research and user feedback, UpEnergy delivers sustainable technology interventions in communities, bridging the gap in climate finance to uplift vulnerable households and combat climate change. UpEnergy believes climate finance has a unique opportunity to directly elevate households living in energy poverty. They exist to fill the gap that often gets overlooked, fighting energy poverty and climate change with projects and technology that benefit those most vulnerable to climate risks.

UpEnergy is searching for one (1) Independent Non-Executive Director to join their board.

The application deadline for this role is 03 May at midnight, 2024.
