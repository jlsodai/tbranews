The excitement is building as we prepare for our highly anticipated Annual Gathering in Egypt this October. Keep reading for essential details and information to help you make the most of this event. From stunning accommodations to travel logistics and safety protocols, we have you covered.


Accommodation

Get ready for an extraordinary experience. While in Cairo, you'll stay at a lavish five-star hotel that provides sophisticated luxury and utmost comfort along with sweeping views of the bustling city. Enjoy the exquisite dining options and rejuvenating spa treatments before your journey to Sharm-el-Sheikh. Once there, immerse yourself in the peaceful ambiance of a tranquil oasis by the Red Sea, boasting opulent amenities.
Arrival

Activities will commence with a welcome dinner on the evening of the 22 October - we would love for you to join us. To ensure you are on time for the welcome dinner, we encourage you to arrive in Cairo by 3:00 PM (Cairo Time).
Airport Shuttle

Complimentary transfers will be available only on the 22 October and at set schedules on those dates. Complete the logistics form to help us determine your shuttle pick-up time and to prepare for your arrival and stay.

COVID Protocol: Passengers travelling to Egypt are not required to take a COVID-19 test. Egypt's COVID-19 entry restrictions were lifted on June 17, 2022. You will need to show proof of COVID vaccination.


Dress Code: The dress code for the reunion is Casual or Smart Casual and Cocktail Attire or Traditional Dress (for the Gala Dinner). Remember that Egypt is a conservative country as you are shopping and packing for the trip. Please carry clothes that cover your shoulders and knees, and do bring a headscarf just in case.


Weather: During this period, the days are usually hot, with balmy evenings. The average daily maximum is 33 C, and the average daily minimum is 23 C.


Currency: The local currency in Egypt is the Egyptian Pound (EGP). ATMs are widely available.
