About the ESG Certificate Programme


The ESG Certificate programme targets women directors and executives looking to develop their understanding of ESG issues and concepts, equipping them with the tools required to drive sustainability agendas in their respective organisations. This expert-led curriculum incorporates ESG theoretical frameworks and trends, situating them within a global context, with practical inputs intended to support executives as they review their roles in driving the ESG agenda.


The programme will give you the tools you need to:


confidently contribute to the complex discourse and debate on ESG issues.

make ESG-related contributions and decisions in the boardroom and executive committees.

develop an ESG strategy that aligns with your organisational goals and values.

enhance your understanding of ESG risks in the context of your operating environment.
