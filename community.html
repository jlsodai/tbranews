TBrA Partners With ARC Ltd to Appoint Two Non-Executive Directors to it’s board


We are proud to have partnered with African Risk Capacity Limited on this board mandate and to have supported the appointments of Helen Amarquaye and Susan Kasinga as Independent Non-Executive Directors to African Risk Capacity’s board of directors.

Susan and Helen bring over 55+ years of combined experience in finance, risk management, and compliance and a demonstrated track record of impact, transformation and delivery.

African Risk Capacity (ARC) is a specialist insurance company established by the African Union to help African governments improve their capacity to better plan for, prepare for and respond to extreme weather events and natural disasters and adapt to climate change.

Audrey Kotey and Gloria Tapon Njamo appointed Non-Executive Directors

Founded in 1970 by Col. Dr. Kwami Nyaho Tamaklo, Nyaho Healthcare Limited is a leader in patient-centered healthcare in Ghana, blending global practices with over fifty years of experience. It houses more than sixty specialists across diverse fields, delivering superior healthcare solutions.

Maria Shipiri and Linda Rogers-Nwokobia appointed to the board of Friendship Bench

Friendship Bench is a global mental health innovator, offering support through community-based "Friendship Benches" where trained volunteers provide culturally-tailored care. They also train community members to enhance mental health access and awareness.

Malado Kaba

Managing Director, Falémé Conseil

2024 International Finance and Development Industry Trends

Malado Kaba, a renowned development economist, shares key industry trends for 2024 in International Finance and Development, highlighting transformative insights. She stresses the importance of macroeconomic policies in championing gender equality and boosting investments in women, particularly in Africa. Boardrooms must now be both adaptable and robust to effectively navigate the relentless legal and regulatory changes characterising the current climate.

Additionally, Malado emphasises the critical role of data in this digital era, advocating for the enhanced collection, analysis, and sharing of quality data, alongside leveraging digital transformation and artificial intelligence (AI). These insights not only spotlight the evolving challenges but also offer a roadmap for navigating the complexities of the global economic landscape. Delve into these forward-thinking perspectives to better understand how they're sculpting the future of the industry.
