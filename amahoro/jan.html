We recently launched Partnerships in Practice, a new series highlighting the critical role the private sector can play in displacement contexts, and the tangible value companies can derive from engaging in these markets. The series shares firsthand examples from our work mobilizing private sector leaders to co-create market-based solutions alongside displaced entrepreneurs.

The first edition featured PSA BDP, a global logistics firm partnering on a program that connects SMEs in displaced communities across Africa with senior executives who share practical experience, tackle real operational challenges, and apply proven business practices. The second edition features Banadir Training Institute (BTI), an institute based in Somalia that trains electrical technicians for the largest power supplier in Mogadishu. We hope you enjoy the series and share it with others to help shape the future of private-sector engagement in displacement contexts.
