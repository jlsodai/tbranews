EMERGE is convening The Future of Work, a virtual town hall, bringing together EMERGE members, employers, partners, and ecosystem stakeholders for a timely and energising conversation on how talent and organisations must evolve in a rapidly changing world.
 
When we talk about the Future of Work, we’re not talking about a distant horizon. We’re talking about the realities shaping careers right now, how technology is redefining roles, how skills are replacing job titles, how careers are becoming less linear, and how purpose, flexibility, and continuous learning are now central to how people choose where and how they work.
 
This interactive session will explore what this shifting world of work means for employers and young professionals today, how organisations can build resilient, future-ready talent systems, and how leaders must rethink culture, capability, and career pathways in an era defined by constant change. Most importantly, it will challenge participants to think differently about how they prepare themselves for what comes next.
