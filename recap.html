Session Recap: Leadership, Value Creation, Strategy & The Board

2025 - Session 1

Session Takeaways


Thank you for your active participation during our during the sessions with Rosalind Kainyah.

In Leadership, Culture & Value creation sessions, we discussed leadership in sustainability and how to create a sustainability culture. We then delved into what factors contribute to value creation and value protection.

In ESG Strategy and the Role of the Board, we examined the four foundations of a sustainability plan and the key elements of implementing an Environment and Social Management System (ESMS) then discussed the board responsibilities in driving ESG.

Finally, we examined the ESG challenges in the Asempa case study and explored the key questions leaders need to ask themselves when determining their ESG Governance Model.

Key Takeaways:

  • Leadership Beyond the Balance Sheet
  • The Both/And Mindset (Marya Besharov)
  • Stakeholder Mapping & Prioritisation
  • Double Materiality
  • Sustainability Maturity Ladder
