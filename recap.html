IoD Virtual Academy

You will also receive access to the IoD Digital Academy on 20 November. This email will come directly from the IoD and not TBrA - please check your inbox or spam regularly and confirm receipt.

The IoD Virtual Academy allows you to access interactive CPD content wherever and whenever you like, allowing you to learn, benchmark and connect on the go and book your live exam prep sessions. It includes bite-size CPD content, articles, podcasts, webinars and videos, all mapped to the Director Competency Framework.
