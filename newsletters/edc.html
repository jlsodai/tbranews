Qualify as a Director with Open Doors

Open Doors, designed and delivered in partnership with the Institute of Directors UK, is a globally recognised and accredited director certificate programme suited to both existing and aspiring directors. The programme provides a comprehensive curriculum leading to a Certificate in Company Direction, a bachelor's level (SCQF) qualification, and the first stage in the Chartered Director qualification pathway.
