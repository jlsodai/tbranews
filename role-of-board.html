Lead Faculty
 
Janhavi Dadarkar holds a portfolio of non-executive directorships and has more than two decades’ experience advising corporates and entrepreneurs globally. She trained as a lawyer before moving into corporate and government affairs, serving as an equity partner in an India-focused advisory firm that she helped build, restructure and successfully exit. A recognised expert in governance and cross-border transactions, Janhavi is a frequent speaker and the author of numerous guides and articles on corporate best practice and joint ventures.
 
 
“Effective boards do more than mitigate risk; they unlock resilience and opportunity. Good directors bring role clarity, uncompromising ethics and practical oversight that converts strategy into lasting value.”
~ Janhavi Dadarkar
