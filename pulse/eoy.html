A Message to Our Community


As we close out 2025, we find ourselves reflecting on a year of remarkable growth, meaningful connections, and the steady building of something truly transformative for leadership development across Africa.

When we launched EMERGE in partnership with TheBoardroom Africa and the Mastercard Foundation, we set out with an ambitious vision: to create a platform where emerging African leaders, talented professionals with 3+ years of experience, could accelerate their journey toward executive leadership. This year, that vision began to take tangible shape.

We've welcomed 1,200 members into our community from across 36 African countries. We've built a community where 71% already manage teams - professionals who aren't just learning about leadership, but living it every day. Through webinars, group coaching sessions, curated content, and peer connections, we've created a space where ambitious professionals can gain clarity on their leadership identity and chart their path forward.

But beyond the numbers, what gives us the greatest pride is the community we're building together. EMERGE is more than a platform, it's an ecosystem where ambitious professionals find peers who understand their aspirations, facilitators and mentors who light the way, and opportunities that match their potential.

To our members: thank you for trusting us with your leadership journey. To our employer partners: thank you for believing in the talent within our community. To our Advisory Committee and the Mastercard Foundation: thank you for your guidance, investment, and unwavering commitment to dignified and fulfilling work for young Africans.

2026 promises to be even more exciting. We're scaling our programmes, deepening our partnerships, and expanding the ways we can serve you. The next generation of African leadership is rising, and you are part of that story.

What's Coming in 2026


If 2025 was about laying the foundation, 2026 is about building at scale. Here's a preview of what's ahead:

Expanded Enterprise Partnerships: New partnerships launching in Q1 2026 with leading organisations in telecommunications, energy, and financial services, creating exclusive leadership development pathways for EMERGE members.

Future of Work Town Hall - February 2026: A flagship event featuring Steve Cadigan, former CHRO at LinkedIn, in conversation with emerging talent and HR leaders across Africa. Save the date!

Expanded Learning Platform: Expanding our integrated offering bringing together assessments, curated content, and peer learning in one seamless experience.

Expanded Group Coaching Cohorts: Multiple cohorts launching throughout the year, for members in various leadership capacities and career stages.

Financial Leadership Content: New programming focused on the financial acumen emerging leaders need - from understanding foundational concepts to board-level financial responsibilities.

Venture-Building Content: Programming for members with ventures of their own, covering funding strategies, investor readiness, and building businesses that scale.
