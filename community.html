Josephine Olok becomes Chartered Director

Open Doors Alumna

Josephine Olok has successfully achieved Chartered Director status, a mark of leadership excellence. Her path to this prestigious credential started with TBrA’s Open Doors programme, developed in partnership with the Institute of Directors (IoD), UK.

Recognised globally, Chartered Director status is an exclusive qualification reserved for the world’s top business leaders and is the only director certification awarded under the Royal Charter. To secure this status, Josephine completed the Certificate in Company Direction through Open Doors, earned the Diploma in Company Direction, and further showcased extensive experience and strong acumen in directorial roles.

We are proud to celebrate Josephine’s milestone achievement and look forward to witnessing her drive more transformation in her leadership capacity.
