TBrA Launches 4th Edition of Egypt Board Diversity Index (BDI) Report

We are proud to present the 4th edition of The Egypt Board Diversity Index report, a comprehensive annual analysis developed by TheBoardroom Africa in collaboration with the Women on Boards Observatory at the American University in Cairo and the Egyptian Exchange.

This edition continues to track and illuminate the progress of gender diversity within the boardrooms of Egypt’s listed companies. Highlighting a sustained upward trend, the report reveals that women’s representation on corporate boards has significantly advanced, with key findings underscoring the strides toward inclusivity and leadership equality in the Egyptian corporate landscape.

Maxine Barnett attains prestigious Chartered Directorship

Maxine Barnett has successfully achieved Chartered Director status, a mark of leadership excellence. Her path to this prestigious credential started with TBrA’s Open Doors programme, developed in partnership with the Institute of Directors (IoD), UK.

Recognised globally, Chartered Director status is an exclusive qualification reserved for the world’s top business leaders and is the only director certification awarded under the Royal Charter. To secure this status, Maxine completed the Certificate in Company Direction through Open Doors, earned the Diploma in Company Direction, and further showcased extensive experience and strong acumen in directorial roles.

We are proud to celebrate Maxine’s milestone achievement and look forward to witnessing her drive more transformation in her leadership capacity.

The programme has been invaluable to my directorship roles, offering rigorous, practical content enriched by skilled instructors and the dynamic exchanges with inspirational women in my cohort.

It has particularly enhanced my ability to distil complex information during board meetings, fostering active listening and strategic conversations. The realistic role-playing exercises not only improved my knowledge but also sharpened my leadership qualities and mindset.

Enhance Your Leadership Circle with Tailored Virtual Connections

How it works

Invitation: Each month, members receive an invitation to participate in Orbiit's matchmaking process through a dedicated link.

Opt-In & Information Sharing: Interested members opt-in by completing a short form, capturing essential personal and professional details, along with their topics of interest.

AI-Powered Matching: After the opt-in deadline, our sophisticated AI algorithm gets to work, analysing the submitted information to create strategic professional matches.

Match Notification: Matches receive an email notification with details of their pairing, suggested virtual session dates, and a conversation guide to foster meaningful dialogue.

Scheduling & Meeting: Orbiit takes care of scheduling the session. Participants then meet for a focused 45-minute virtual session, designed to inspire and empower.

Feedback: Post-session, participants are encouraged to share their feedback, helping to refine and enhance the matchmaking experience.
