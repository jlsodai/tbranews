Expanding Connections Through Peer Matching


Ashwin West
Head, Sustainable Infrastructure Investments
BlueOrchard Finance Ltd

TheBoardroom Africa’s Peer Matching initiative continues to foster meaningful connections among leaders across industries.

Ashwin West shares his experience:

“TheBoardroom Africa’s Peer Matching experience was truly enriching. I had the opportunity to connect with a professional in Nigeria’s energy access sector, and our discussion was a dynamic exchange of insights. While she was eager to learn from my experiences as a director on African boards, I was equally interested in understanding how her company is navigating the complexities of energy access in Nigeria. It was a valuable two-way learning experience that reinforced the power of meaningful connections.

This initiative provides an excellent platform to expand networks, share knowledge, and gain new perspectives from leaders across industries. I highly recommend it to anyone looking to engage with like-minded professionals and look forward to participating again!”


How it works

  • Invitation: Each month, members receive an invitation to participate in Orbiit's matchmaking process through a dedicated link.
  • Opt-In & Information Sharing: Interested members opt-in by completing a short form, capturing essential personal and professional details, along with their topics of interest.
  • AI-Powered Matching: After the opt-in deadline, our sophisticated AI algorithm gets to work, analysing the submitted information to create strategic professional matches.
  • Match Notification: Matches receive an email notification with details of their pairing, suggested virtual session dates, and a conversation guide to foster meaningful dialogue.
  • Scheduling & Meeting: The platform takes care of scheduling the session. Participants then meet for a focused 45-minute virtual session, designed to inspire and empower.
  • Feedback: Post-session, participants are encouraged to share their feedback, helping to refine and enhance the peer matching experience.

Ensure you’re part of this month’s peer-matching by opting in. Look out for the monthly opt-in invitations and seize the opportunity to connect with like-minded professionals.

Essential Skills for the Modern Board Director

Shefaly Yogendra
CMBD Faculty

Move Beyond Compliance – Forward-thinking boards focus on strategy, innovation, and competitiveness, not just governance.

Embrace Cognitive Diversity – Diverse perspectives enrich board discussions but require strong collaboration skills.

Master Boardroom Dynamics – Build productive relationships with colleagues you see only a few times a year.

Disagree Constructively – Challenge ideas respectfully and educate peers without being condescending.

Stay Independent & Informed – Avoid groupthink while continuously expanding your governance expertise.
