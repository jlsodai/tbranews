Why Egypt?


Egypt is built on the history of powerful women—Cleopatra, Nefertiti, Hatshepsut— who reigned and ruled in balance. One of the cradles of civilisation and a hub for African business, we chose Egypt as an ode to this rich history, a centre for culture and commerce, and an opportunity to increase and transform our presence globally by fostering cross-cultural exchange between North Africa, Sub-Saharan Africa and beyond.


Experience the vibrant energies of Cairo & Sharm-el-Sheikh. Soak in the culture, the hustle and bustle of the capital city before we venture south to the resort town of Sharm-el-Sheikh for an immersive experience.
