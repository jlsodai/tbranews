Message from the CEO

As we come to the end of 2025, I’ve been reflecting on a year defined by focus, consolidation, and steady progress. One of the most significant milestones in our journey was becoming a Certified B Corporation. Following a rigorous assessment process, we joined a global community of businesses that meet high standards of social and environmental impact, transparency, and accountability. This independent certification affirms the values that underpin how we lead, operate, and build for the long term.

Finding executive and board leaders for some of the most transformative businesses and organisations in Africa and globally remained central to our work. We deepened partnerships across key markets, supporting organisations navigating increasingly complex leadership needs, from multinational fintechs and impact investors to luxury food brands. Our commitment to rigorous market mapping, global reach, and disciplined execution, combined with a strong focus on value-adding search delivery, has led clients to increasingly entrust us with their most critical and strategic C-suite and executive mandates.

Alongside this, our membership community continued to grow in depth and capability. Leaders benefited from expanded development and professional support, including coaching, governance and ESG training, as well as virtual peer connections. We further strengthened the value of our community through a robust programme of thought leadership, delivering data, insight, and practical perspectives via our Industry Trends Report, our collaboration with McKinsey on Women in the Workplace, the launch of Pulse, and delivering new depth and insights to our Board Diversity Index Reports.

As we look ahead to 2026, we will be a decade old. We enter it with deep gratitude to our team, members, friends and partners, and with a clear sense of what the next 10 years of leadership on the continent will demand and deliver.

Here’s to rest, renewal, and the possibilities of a new year.
