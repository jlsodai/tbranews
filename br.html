Norsad Capital

Non-Executive Director


Industry: Financial Services

Norsad, established in 1990 as a collaboration between Nordic and SADC governments, is a multilateral agency based in Zambia. It transformed into Norsad Finance, a limited liability company, in 2011 and rebranded as Norsad Capital in 2022 to expand its impact in Africa. Norsad Capital is an impact investor and private credit provider that offers debt and mezzanine finance to mid-market growth companies. Their tailor-made debt finance solutions enable these companies to have substantial and ongoing social impact through their services and employment. With this, Norsad Capital aims to positively impact the lives of 100 million Africans by 2030.

Norsad Capital is searching for 1 (one) Non-Executive Director to join their board as a replacement for a retired director.

The application deadline for this role is 31 July at midnight, 2023.

The Fred Hollows Foundation Kenya

Non-Executive Directors


Industry: Non-Profit

The Fred Hollows Foundation Kenya is part of a global group, with a presence in 25 countries worldwide, dedicated to ending avoidable blindness and treating vision impairment. They ensure equitable access to high-quality eye care services for all Kenyans, prioritising advocacy and collaboration with governments and NGOs. With a comprehensive approach and a focus on sustainability, they strive to prevent blindness and restore sight. Their global governance roadmap empowers local directors for meaningful impact at the local level.

The Fred Hollows Foundation Kenya is searching for 2 Non-Executive Directors to join their board.

The application deadline for this role is 18 August at midnight, 2023.

Financing Alliance for Health

Non-Executive Directors


Industry: Health Financing

Financing Alliance for Health aims to bridge the funding gap of over $4 billion for community health systems in Sub-Saharan Africa. They advocate for shifting from costly and inefficient vertical programmes to integrated ones. The alliance works with governments to overcome barriers and develop financing strategies, enabling the mobilisation of resources for robust and sustainable community health systems. Their goal is to prioritise community health as a crucial investment for achieving universal health coverage and improving health outcomes.

Financing Alliance for Health is searching for 3 (three) Non-Executive Directors to join their board; 1 Co-Chair and 2 Non-Executive Directors.

The application deadline for this role is 20 July at midnight, 2023.
