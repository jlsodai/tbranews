About the webinar

Pursuing meaningful impact has become paramount in the ever-evolving landscape of business leadership. According to the Financial Times, thought leadership "in its purest form consists of genuine insight that cuts through the clutter and has a real, lasting impact on its audience."


For corporate executives, one platform stands tall: LinkedIn - the quintessential thought leadership channel for professional growth. Notably, pages on LinkedIn that post weekly enjoy a staggering 5.6 times more followers than those that post monthly. It is easy to see how the combination of Thought Leadership + LinkedIn can be a powerful way of gaining influence and impact.

The rise of LinkedIn and the role thought leadership plays in helping business leaders share their values, expand their network and reach their personal goals.



Join the session to explore
  • The significance of Thought Leadership in enhancing your influence and impact
  • Why LinkedIn is such a crucial publishing platform
  • How to expand your network through content
  • Why many time-pressed business leaders enlist help to manage their digital thought leadership and content strategy


About Lysanne Currie

Lysanne Currie is CEO and Editor in Chief at Meet the Leader Ltd. She founded the company, a collective of specialist journalists and content creators, to help business leaders tell their stories through ghost-writing, print, video and online. Formerly Editorial Director at Hachette UK, Editor in Chief at Sky and most recently Head of Content at the IoD and Editor in Chief of their glossy business magazine, Director, she continues to edit and write for a variety of business publications, including CityAM, CA Magazine, Influence, Tempus, Robb Report UK, Said Business School Review and Investor Magazine.
