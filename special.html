LinkedIn’s first CHRO, Steve Cadigan, Joins TheBoardroom Africa’s Board of Directors

TheBoardroom Africa (TBrA) is pleased to announce the appointment of Steve Cadigan to its board of directors. As LinkedIn’s first Chief Human Resources Officer (CHRO) and a renowned talent strategist and author of the groundbreaking book ‘Workquake,’ Steve brings a wealth of experience and insight that will be pivotal as TBrA advances its mission to deliver top, diverse leadership talent at scale and provide the tools they need to transform the businesses they lead.

Steve’s leadership and influence have been recognised globally, with Stanford University developing a graduate-level course inspired by his work in shaping LinkedIn’s culture. His book, Workquake: Embracing the Aftershocks of COVID-19 to Create a Better Model of Working, published in 2021, underscores his thought leadership on the future of work.

As TheBoardroom Africa advances its mission to transform leaders and their businesses, we’re increasingly aware of the pivotal period we’re in and the ever-complex and ever-changing nature of leadership, which has been transformed, among others, by technology. We’re excited about the role Steve will play as a strategic advisor to TheBoardroom Africa, in helping us unlock the intersections between talent, technology and sustainability.

Bola Tinubu, Chair of TheBoardroom Africa, added, "Steve Cadigan's deep expertise and innovative mindset makes him an invaluable addition to our board. His contributions will undoubtedly enhance our decision-making, and our ability to lead and influence in this ever-evolving leadership landscape."

Commenting on his appointment, Steve Cadigan said, "when you find an organisation that has a purpose and mission so closely connected to your own, you immediately seek to forge a deep connection. I am honored to join TBrA’s board today and will do my best to make this great organisation even greater."

“We are thrilled to welcome Steve Cadigan to TBrA’s Board. Steve's remarkable expertise in talent strategy and leadership development aligns perfectly with our mission to transform corporate leadership spaces in Africa. His deep experience with global talent dynamics and innovative approaches will be invaluable as we scale our technology to deliver our solutions at scale. I look forward to his advice and support, and to continuing to work with the board to advance our strategy.” said Marcia Ashong-Sam, CEO of TheBoardroom Africa.

Please join us in congratulating Steve on his appointment and we invite you to learn more more about his work and impact.
