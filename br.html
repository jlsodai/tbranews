MTN Mobile Money Benin SA

Non-Executive Director


Regional Opportunity

Industry: Fintech

MTN Mobile Money Benin SA (MTN MoMo Benin SA) is a company specialising in the provision of digital financial products and services; it is the leader in the Benin mobile money market with over 70% market share and 88% value share. The mobile money wallet is mainly used to transfer value from one person to another (P2P) for payment of goods and services such as buying airtime, paying utility bills, salaries of some workers, taxi fares, micro-credit, savings, and micro-insurance, subscribing to services, etc.

The company is part of MTN Group, an emerging market mobile operator at the forefront of technological and digital changes, providing a diverse range of voice, data, digital, fintech, wholesale and enterprise services to more than 277 million customers in 20 markets.

MTN MoMo Benin SA is searching for one (1) Non-Executive Director to join its board.

The application deadline for this role is 14 July at midnight, 2024.

MedAccess

Non-Executive Director


Global Opportunity

Industry: Global Health

MedAccess is an independent, not-for-profit organisation dedicated to improving access to essential medications for over two billion people worldwide who lack these treatments, thereby reducing preventable illness and mortality.

Operating in over 95 countries, MedAccess facilitates access to vital vaccines, medications, diagnostics, and technologies for marginalised communities through brokering and financing agreements. Supported by a £200 million capital infusion from British International Investment, it ensures lower prices and reliable medical supply chains.

MedAccess is searching for one (1) Non-Executive Director to join its board.

The application deadline for this role is 26 July at midnight, 2024.

ARM-Harith Infrastructure Investment Limited

Independent Non-Executive Director


Regional Opportunity

Industry: Private Equity

ARM-Harith is a joint venture between South Africa’s premier Pan-African infrastructure fund manager, Harith General Partners, and Nigeria’s largest non-bank financial services provider, ARM Limited. The company invests in transformative energy and infrastructure projects that boost economies, create jobs, and enhance communities across Africa. With expertise in sectors like energy, transport, and utilities, ARM-Harith drives sustainable development and long-term growth. Their pioneering initiatives in West Africa and beyond reshape the infrastructure landscape, benefiting investors, local communities, and the global environment while ensuring resource protection for future generations.

ARM-Harith is searching for one (1) Independent Non-Executive Director to join its board.

The application deadline for this role is 11 July at midnight, 2024.

Non-Executive Director


Regional Opportunity

Industry: FMCG

MDaaS Global

Non-Executive Director


Regional Opportunity

Industry: Healthcare

MDaaS, founded in 2016, is a healthcare company that builds and operates modern, technology-enabled diagnostic centres for underserved communities. Initially selling medical equipment, they pivoted to diagnostics in 2017 after recognising the severe shortage of such services in Africa. Today, they provide radiology, cardiac, and laboratory services to over 120,000 patients through 66,000 tests conducted across their 17 centres, which include full-service, embedded, and sample collection centres in eight states. MDaaS's flagship centre in Ibadan spearheaded their expansion, improving healthcare access for millions in Nigeria.

Mdaas Global is searching for one (1) Non-Executive Director to join their board.

The application deadline for this role is 4 July at midnight, 2024.
