Session Takeaways

It was great having you on Day One of the Role of the Director and the Board.


Janhavi defined Corporate Governance as the rules, processes, systems, or structures by which any organisation is directed, led, or controlled. This definition provided an excellent foundation for the rest of the sessions, where we delved into what constitutes an organisation, its stakeholders, and the responsibilities of these stakeholders; where we looked into the fundamental roles of shareholders, executives, and the board with respect to the organisation and how it is led and governed.


Key Takeaways:


  • Different types of entities
  • Key duties, roles and legal responsibilities of the director
  • The differences between direction, management and ownership
  • The strategies for delivering best practices in the boardroom
  • The board’s role in corporate governance and improving board effectiveness

Please remember to study the G20/EOD Principles in preparation for taking the module exam.
