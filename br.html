TBrA Appointments

Congratulations to Anne Mwasi on her appointment as Non-Executive Director on the board of VisionFund Zambia Limited; a deposit-taking Microfinance Institution licensed by the Bank of Zambia. It is part of the VisionFund International Network, the microfinance arm of World Vision, which is the largest Christian relief, development, and advocacy organisation in the world.
 

Appointed as

Non-Executive Director,
VisionFund Zambia Limited

Anne has 20+ years of experience in the Financial and Public sectors, notably as Chief Finance Officer at JamiiBora Microfinance, leading its transformation into JamiiBora Bank after its acquisition by City Finance Bank. She's been a board member at The Institute of Internal Auditors and presently serves as a Non-Executive Director at Yehu Microfinance Services, actively contributing to the Audit, Social Performance, and Sustainability Committees. Additionally, she holds the position of Board Secretary at the Nyamira County Public Service Board.

Anne Mwasi

Featured Opportunity
Closes: 13 October 2023

African Risk Capacity Insurance Company Ltd

Financial Services


Industry: Non-Executive Directors

African Risk Capacity Insurance Company Ltd (ARC Ltd), an affiliate of African Risk Capacity (AU Agency), serves African governments and communities in reducing the financial impact of natural disasters through insurance and timely payment of claims. Backed by $100M from UK FCDO and Germany BMZ through KFW, it is a mutual insurer owned by African sovereigns, functioning as a pioneering public-private partnership to enhance economic growth in Africa and empower governments to safeguard their citizens.

ARC Ltd is searching for 2 (two) Non-Executive Directors representing member countries to join their board as replacements for retiring directors.

The application deadline for this role is 13 October at midnight, 2023.
