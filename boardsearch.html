MTN Ghana - Mobile Money Limited

Application Deadline

13th January 2023

Relevant Industry

Fintech

MTN is an emerging market mobile operator at the forefront of technological and digital changes. They provide a diverse range of voice, data, digital, fintech, wholesale and enterprise services to more than 277m customers in 20 markets. The company was established in South Africa at the dawn of democracy, in 1994, as a leader in transformation. Since then, it has grown by investing in sophisticated communication infrastructure, developing new technologies and by harnessing the talents of the diverse team of people to now offer services to communities across Africa and the Middle East.


On 31 May 2018, MTN Ghana launched its initial public offering (IPO). The IPO closed on 31 July 2019. A total of up to 4,637,394,533 ordinary shares of MTN Ghana, representing 35% of its equity was offered to qualifying applicants. This was part of the agreement between MTN Ghana and Ghana's National Communications Authority (NCA) in November 2015 for MTN Ghana to deploy 4G LTE mobile services to its customers in Ghana. Among other methods of payment, MTN Mobile Money was included as a payment option for the MTN share offer subscription. This was the first time mobile money had been used as a payment method in an IPO. The mobile money wallet is mainly used to transfer value from one person to another person (P2P), for payment of goods and services such as buying airtime, paying for utility bills, Gold and DSTV bills, salaries of some workers, taxi fares, micro-credit, savings and micro-insurance.


MTN Ghana's Mobile Money Limited is searching for 3 (three) Independent Non-Executive Directors to join its board; one Independent Chair, and two Independent Non-Executive Directors who will also serve as Chairs of the Audit & Risk, and Human Resource and Nomination Committees.


The application deadline for this role is 13th January at midnight, 2023.

Acacia Innovations Limited

Application Deadline

10th February 2023

Relevant Industry

Renewable Energy

Founded in 2016, Acacia Innovations Ltd. is a social enterprise which provides environmentally friendly and cost-saving cooking and fuel solutions for schools and small businesses in Kenya. They manufacture and distribute Kuni Safi briquettes, a nearly smokeless alternative to firewood made from sugarcane waste. Acacia Innovations Ltd. is also the largest supplier of clean cooking fuels to schools in Kenya, with 500+ school and small-business customers. They have been internationally recognised as a Winner of the Start-Up Energy Transition Award sponsored by the German Government and as a recipient of an Energy and Environment Project grant from the Nordic Development Fund.


Acacia Innovations Ltd. has a female founder and a staff of more than 60% women. The social enterprise reached breakeven in 2022, and aims to operate as a profitable business while maintaining a high social and environmental impact.


Acacia Innovations Ltd. is searching for 1 (one) Independent Non-Executive Director to join its board of directors as a Chairperson.


The application deadline for this role is 10th February at midnight, 2023.
