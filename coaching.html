Meet Your 2025 Coaches

Marjorie Ngwenya
Leadership Coach | Author | Non-executive Director

Marjorie is an experienced coach who works with clients to navigate their personal and professional lives authentically and overcome their barriers to success. She is an external member of the Prudential Regulation Committee of the Bank of England.

She is also the chairperson of the Canon Collins Trust and a trustee of the Legal Resources Centre. She is a past President of the Institute and Faculty of Actuaries (IFoA) and was the first person of colour to hold office in the organisation’s 160 years and also its youngest.

 

Her prior roles include being a member of the Group Executive Committee of Liberty Group in South Africa as Chief Strategist. Prior to that, she was the Chief Risk Officer of Old Mutual’s African Operations. Marjorie trained as an actuary and holds a Sloan Masters in Leadership and Strategy from the London Business School.

LOUISE NEWTON
Founder & Director: Career Therapy
Career Coach & Professional Branding Specialist

Louise is the founder of Career Therapy, a coaching practice where she specialises in helping executives build their career confidence and presence. Blending approaches from positive psychology and cognitive behavioural and strengths-based coaching, she helps her clients identify and overcome their limiting beliefs to transform self-doubt into self-confidence.

With over 12 years of specialised practice in executive coaching, leadership development and professional branding, she helps clients understand who they are now, who they want to be next and what they want to be known for, developing their brand and growing their ability to communicate and influence.

 

Her professional and commercial background is a blend of 20+ years of public and private sector experience, culminating in a Vice President role at Goldman Sachs before launching both a coaching and a change management consulting practice. A Chartered Member of the CIPD, ICF qualified with over 4,500 hours of career and business coaching experience, she has helped 100s of clients boost their confidence and professional presence and accelerate their careers.

LAUREN DAVIS
Mental Health Coach | Clinical Psychologist | Workplace Wellbeing Strategist

Lauren is a Clinical Psychologist and Mental Health Coach with over two decades of experience supporting leaders and organisations in building mentally healthy, high-performing workplaces. She has collaborated with thousands of leaders and over 100 companies, bringing a unique perspective - blending clinical expertise with organisational strategies to enhance workplace well-being

As the founder of Lauren Davis Wellbeing, she specialises in leadership mental health, workplace resilience, and fostering psychologically safe cultures. Lauren delivers impactful keynotes, workshops, and masterclasses on critical topics, including Beyond Burnout, Psychological Safety, and Leading Through Change. As a certified Time To Think facilitator, she applies Nancy Kline's principles to create environments that enhance critical thinking and decision-making.

 

Lauren is dedicated to helping individuals, teams, and organisations discover their purpose and fulfil their potential. Her passion lies in supporting individuals to achieve both personal and professional excellence. Her firm yet empathic approach transforms clients from passive recipients to active participants in their lives, making her a trusted ally for those seeking to enhance both work performance and overall well-being.

SHUNGU CHIRUNDA
Integral Executive Coach | Leadership & Culture Transformation Specialist | Pracademic

Shungu is an Executive Coach (PCC, ICF), Entrepreneur and the Founder of SenseSana, a multidisciplinary practice shaping future-relevant organisations through human-centred interventions. Shungu has a background in Human Rights Law and a deep commitment to empowerment, equality and African futures.

Her coaching practice focuses on decision-makers, leaders and C-suite teams only. Specialising in responsible leadership, leadership embodiment, resilience, and culture transformation in hybrid workplaces. With many years of executive leadership experience across international corporations and start-ups, Shungu serves on the boards of entities that are purposed to give access to better opportunities for individuals and under-served / marginalised communities. She is an advisor and thinking partner to Founders on the African Continent. She is also Chairperson of the African Crowdfunding Association (ACfA) and Chairperson of the Board of Kuunda Holdings.

 

Shungu is a part-time faculty at the University of Cape Town’s Graduate School of Business and is a Course Leader at The Centre for Coaching. She holds a Master of Laws (LL.M) in Human Rights Law from SOAS, University of London. She also holds a certification from MIT in Future Commerce. Likewise, certifications in Breathwork Coaching, Leadership Assessments, Organisational Culture Assessment and Resilience.
