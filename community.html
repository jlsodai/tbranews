Unveiling Our New Digital Home: Explore TBrA's New Website


We’re excited to unveil TheBoardroom Africa’s brand-new website!

As we continue to scale and enhance our services, this new digital home captures our plans to leverage cutting-edge technology to deliver diverse leadership talent at scale and revolutionise executive education across Africa and beyond. The intuitive interface provides seamless access to our curated leadership search solutions and executive education offerings, showcasing the value we deliver to clients and the transformative impact we bring to leadership and industries.

TBrA partners with MedAccess, FINCA DRC and Norsad Capital on a new appointments

Angela Gichaga
Non-Executive Director
MedAccess

We are excited to have partnered with MedAccess to appoint Angela Gichaga, Global President and CEO of the Financing Alliance for Health, as its new non-executive director.

Dr Gichaga is an esteemed public and social sector leader with extensive experience across civil service, management consulting, NGOs, and academia.

Read more about the appointment


Vusi Raseroka
Chief Executive Officer
Norsad Capital

We are pleased to have partnered with Norsad Capital to support the appointment of Vusi Raseroka as Chief Executive Officer.

We extend our warmest congratulations to him and look forward to witnessing the positive impact he will deliver in his new role.

Read more about the appointment


Noella Mukoka
Independent Non-Executive Director
FINCA DRC

We are proud to have partnered with FINCA R.D CONGO SA, a Membre du Groupe FINCA International Inc. et FINCA Microfinance Holding Company LLC, and to have supported the appointment of Noella Mukoka as another local Congolese and Independent Non-Executive Director on the Board of Directors of FINCA DRC.

We congratulate Noella Mukoka on this extraordinary appointment and wish her all the very best in her new role.
