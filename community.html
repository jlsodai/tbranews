Programme Highlights: Open Doors in Cape Town


Last month, Open Doors returned in-person for the first time since the COVID pandemic. The thirteenth cohort gathered in Cape Town, South Africa, where the picturesque Arabella Country Estate provided a breathtaking backdrop for the training retreat. This immersive experience spanned six days and focused on community building and intensive learning. We also welcomed the inaugural cohort of Open Doors Diploma delegates, who joined us to continue their journey towards becoming Chartered Directors.

We are proud to have successfully concluded 13 sessions of Open Doors, which has, since inception, graduated over 260 women leaders, both existing and aspiring board directors, equipping them with the tools needed to effectively lead in the boardroom. Our gratitude extends to our enduring partnership with the Institute of Directors, UK (IoD), and the dedicated graduates whose collective pathways continue to reinforce the need to ensure more women have the tools to transform their boardrooms. We are proud to witness the ongoing global impact our Open Doors community achieves.

Conscious Existence
