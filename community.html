Celebrating
Dr. Nancy Onyango


We're celebrating TBrA member Dr. Nancy Onyango as she concludes her remarkable tenure at the International Monetary Fund (IMF). Dr Onyango led the IMF’s Office of Internal Audit and Inspection since 2018; during her tenure, she reinforced transparency and accountability and led pioneering initiatives. An advocate for gender quality and women’s empowerment, her accomplishments include the first-ever audit of the IMF’s risk management framework and introducing the IMF’s inaugural mental health strategy.

We celebrate her achievements and look forward to her next chapter.

Gender Inclusivity, A Catalyst for Decarbonisation in the Cement Industry


Delve into this insightful publication on the cement industry, a collaborative effort by investment and consulting firms—Africa Ahead and Radiant ESG. This comprehensive analysis explores the crucial connection between women's involvement and decarbonisation while illuminating best practices concerning gender-related policies, data collection, and reporting.

“In this paper, we establish a baseline understanding of the current state of women’s participation in the Cement industry by revealing the composition of the women workforce and sharing what we have learned about the link between geographic location, reporting, and gender representation.” - TBrA member Denise Ntonta, Co-Founder of Africa Ahead
