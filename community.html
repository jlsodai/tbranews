In Conversation: Unlocking Nigeria's Energy Potential

In a compelling discussion, TheBoardroom Africa members Rolake Akinkugbe-Filani and Olu Verheijen explored Nigeria’s ambitious strategy to transform its energy sector and attract investment.

Olu Verheijen, Special Adviser to President Bola Tinubu on Energy, detailed the government’s priorities, including boosting oil production to 4 million barrels per day by 2030, enhancing upstream fiscal policies, and addressing security challenges.

With Nigeria’s energy transition at the forefront, the conversation provided key insights into policy shifts, investment opportunities, and the future of energy security in Africa.
